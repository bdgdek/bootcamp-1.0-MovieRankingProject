The Best Movies According to Dek

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Don't Breathe

A group of friends breaks into the house of a blind man,
hoping to steal his fortune.
But their perfect crime turns into a nightmare when they discover their victim is far more dangerous than they imagined — and he’s not as helpless as he seems.

Truth or Dare

A harmless game of Truth or Dare among friends turns deadly
when a mysterious force punishes anyone who lies or refuses a dare.
What begins as fun quickly spirals into a terrifying fight for survival,
where every choice could be their last.

Rush Hour

When a Chinese diplomat’s daughter is kidnapped in Los Angeles,
Hong Kong Detective Inspector Lee teams up with fast-talking LAPD cop James Carter.
Their clashing styles and nonstop banter lead to a wild mix of action and comedy
as they race against time to rescue the girl and crack the case.
